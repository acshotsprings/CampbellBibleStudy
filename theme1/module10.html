Module 10 — Theme 1: End Times

Signs of the Times

Read first
Matthew 24:1–14, 2 Timothy 3:1–5, Daniel 12:4

Jesus told His disciples to recognize the signs of the times — events of prophetic significance that indicate the Tribulation is drawing near. These signs do not set dates, but they tell us the stage is being set.

🙏 Reflection & Prayer
Jesus called these signs "birth pains" — they intensify and accelerate before the end. What does it feel like to live in a season of accelerating birth pains?
Of the signs listed above, which ones do I personally witness most clearly in my own community or family?
Daniel 12:4 says knowledge will increase dramatically in the last days — we clearly live in that moment. How does rapid technological change make me more or less dependent on God?
