📗 Prophetic Fiction

Stories that dramatize what Scripture teaches about the end times. Fiction is not Scripture — but well-written prophetic fiction can make the geopolitics, the timelines, and the tribulation feel real in a way pure doctrine sometimes can't.

Navigate: ← Back to Resource Library · Prophecy & Eschatology → · Biblical Calendar →

A word on fiction: These novels take biblical prophecy seriously and dramatize it in modern settings. They are not inspired, and their details are the authors' — not Scripture's. Read them as imagination anchored in real theology, not as prediction. When a detail surprises you, check it against the actual biblical text.
