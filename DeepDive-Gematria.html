🛡️ Biblical Discernment Framework

While studying gematria, we must maintain biblical balance. Numbers can reveal patterns, but we must avoid:

  • Making doctrine from number patterns alone
  • Forcing correlations where none exist
  • Prioritizing numerical meaning over clear biblical text
  • Engaging in speculation rather than scriptural study
  • Treating gematria as mystical rather than mathematical
⚠️ Study Guidelines

Remember: Scripture interprets Scripture. Any insights from gematria must align with clear biblical teaching. Use this as a study tool, not a source of new revelation.
