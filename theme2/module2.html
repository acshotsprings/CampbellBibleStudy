Creation
~4000 BC
God's Original Calendar — The 364-Day Solar Year
God's Design · Genesis 1–2
God created the sun, moon, and stars on Day 4 specifically "for signs, seasons, days, and years" (Gen 1:14). The Essenes, the Book of Enoch, and the Book of Jubilees all agree: the original year was exactly 364 days — 52 complete weeks. Every feast, every Sabbath fell on the same day of the week, every year. The year began at the spring equinox. The Essenes believed this calendar was observed unbroken from Adam through David.
364-Day Solar God-Given
Noah's Flood
~2348 BC
Calendar Integrity Maintained Through the Flood
Jubilees 6:36–38 · Enoch 72:32
The Essenes addressed the obvious question: did the Flood alter the Earth's orbit and break the calendar? Their answer was no — the tropical year (365.2422 days) remained unchanged before and after the Flood. The Dead Sea Scrolls record that Noah commanded his children to observe the solstices and equinoxes as days of remembrance. The 364-day calendar continued, with Noah himself using it.
364-Day Solar
The Exodus
~1446 BC
God Resets the New Year — Nisan as Month 1
Exodus 12:1–2 · Leviticus 23
At the moment of redemption from Egypt, God told Moses: "This month shall be the first month of the year for you." The spring month of Nisan — the month of Passover — became the anchor of the covenantal calendar. Leviticus 23 then established all seven appointed feasts (moedim) tied to this structure. The original solar calendar and the new covenantal framework were unified.
Solar Foundation Covenantal Reset
Seleucid Invasion
~175–164 BC
Antiochus Epiphanes Forces the Seleucid Greek Calendar
The First Calendar Corruption
Antiochus Epiphanes imposed the Seleucid Greek lunar calendar on the Jewish people — changing New Year's Day from spring (Nisan) to fall (Tishrei), and calculating months by the crescent moon rather than the solstices and equinoxes. This is the calendar that directly conflicted with what the Zadokite priests had preserved. The Essenes identified this as the beginning of a catastrophic corruption.

📌 Who were the Zadokite priests? The Zadokites (also called the Sons of Zadok) were the priestly family that had held the Jerusalem high priesthood in an unbroken line since Solomon appointed Zadok as his chief priest (~950 BC). For roughly 800 years, every high priest in the Temple descended from this one family. They guarded not just the Temple rituals but the sacred 364-day solar calendar, which they believed had been revealed by God and maintained by angels. When Antiochus IV came to power in 175 BC, he removed the legitimate Zadokite high priest (Onias III) — first by installing Onias's own brother Jason in exchange for a bribe, then by replacing Jason with Menelaus, who was not even a Zadokite. Onias III was eventually assassinated. The Zadokite priests who refused to accept the new lunar calendar and the corrupt priestly succession withdrew to the Judean desert at Qumran — and the scrolls they took with them became the Dead Sea Scrolls.
354-Day Lunar Forced Corruption
Maccabean Revolt
164 BC
The Maccabees Win — But Keep the Greek Calendar
The Unfinished Restoration
The Maccabees drove out the Greeks and rededicated the Temple (Hanukkah). They reinstated Mosaic law — but they never reinstated the original solar calendar. As part of their political settlement they retained the Seleucid-influenced lunar calendar. The Zadokite priests who refused this compromise withdrew to Qumran, taking the Temple library with them. This is the origin of the Dead Sea Scrolls community — and the Pharisee/Essene split.
Compromise Lunar Calendar Kept
Qumran Community
~150 BC – 68 AD
The Essenes Preserve the Original Calendar at Qumran
Dead Sea Scrolls · Sons of Light vs. Sons of Darkness
The Essenes — calling themselves the Sons of Light — preserved the original 364-day solar calendar in their community at Qumran. They called the Pharisees the "Sons of Darkness" for following the lunar calendar. The Dead Sea Scrolls contain numerous calendar texts: 4Q318, 4Q394, the Damascus Document, the Community Rule. They believed that high priests performing Yom Kippur on the wrong day (under the corrupt calendar) were dying in God's presence — the Talmud (Yoma 9a) records over 300 high priests in 420 years died within a year of taking office.
364-Day Solar Preserved
Julius Caesar
46 BC
The Julian Calendar — 365-Day Solar with Leap Year
Civil / Roman Calendar
Julius Caesar reformed the Roman calendar in 46 BC, creating the Julian calendar — a 365-day solar year with a leap day every four years. This calendar restored the 7-day week structure that Rome had briefly abandoned for an 8-day cycle. The Essenes noted that the Julian calendar at least preserved the same seven-day week they used. Josephus wrote that the Romans referred to the Jews as keeping their own "Saturnalia" (day of rest) every Saturday — confirming the Sabbath was Saturday in the 1st century.
365-Day Solar Civil
Hillel II
~358–359 AD
Rabbi Hillel II Standardizes the Modern Jewish Calendar
The Calendar the Church Has Used Ever Since
After the destruction of Jerusalem (70 AD) scattered Jewish communities across the world, it became impossible to coordinate the calendar by observation of the crescent moon from Jerusalem. Around 358–359 AD, Rabbi Hillel II codified a standardized mathematical formula — the Jewish calendar still in use today. His rules moved certain holy days to avoid falling on Sabbaths. This is a modification of the Pharisee lunar calendar, not the original solar calendar — and even by strict Pharisee standards it is not entirely accurate.
354-Day Lunar (Modified)
Gregorian Reform
1582 AD
The Gregorian Calendar — Julian Corrected for Leap Year Drift
Current Civil Calendar
By the Middle Ages the Julian calendar's leap year calculation had caused the spring equinox to drift 11 days. Pope Gregory XIII authorized the Gregorian calendar in 1582, correcting the leap year formula while preserving the identical seven-day week structure. When countries adopted it, they skipped days but deliberately preserved the weekly cycle. In America (1752): Wednesday September 2 was followed by Thursday September 14 — Saturday remained Saturday. The seven-day week has been unbroken from creation.
365-Day Solar Civil 7-Day Week Preserved
The Millennium
Future
Christ's Reign — The Original Calendar Restored
Isaiah 66:23 · Ezekiel 46:1 · Revelation 20
Both Isaiah and Ezekiel prophesy that in the Millennial Kingdom, all humanity will observe the same Sabbaths and new moons in worship before God. The Enoch calendar states that these cycles do not change until the new heaven and new earth (Enoch 72:1). The calendar God designed at creation is the calendar that will structure worship during Christ's 1,000-year reign from Jerusalem. The original design was never abandoned by God — only by man.
Millennial Restoration Original Cycles Restored
